1. Getting Started

How Account Creation Works

EduLMS does not use a traditional registration with password. Instead:

  1. You fill the registration form (no password field).
  2. A 6-digit OTP is sent to your email — enter it to verify your address.
  3. Your account is created with Pending status. A Teacher (for students) or the Admin (for teachers) must approve it.
  4. Once approved, you receive a unique password setup link by email. Click it to set your own password.
  5. The link is valid for 72 hours and can only be used once.

⚠️ Important: You cannot log in until your account is approved AND you have set your password via the email link.

Logging In

  1. Go to /accounts/login/
  2. Enter your username and password
  3. You will be redirected to your role's dashboard automatically

Forgot Your Password?

  1. Click "Forgot password?" on the login page
  2. Enter your registered email address
  3. Check your inbox for a reset link (valid 1 hour)
  4. Click the link and set a new password

2. Student Guide

Step 1 — Register

  1. Go to /accounts/register/
  2. Fill in: First Name, Last Name, Username, Email, Role (Student), Grade Level
  3. Click Continue to Email Verification
  4. Check your email for a 6-digit OTP code and enter it on the next page
  5. Wait for a teacher to approve your account — you will receive an email when approved

Step 2 — Set Your Password

  1. When a teacher approves your account, an email is sent with a password setup link
  2. Click the link in the email (check spam if not in inbox)
  3. Set a strong password (at least 8 characters, mix letters, numbers, symbols)
  4. You will be automatically logged in after setting your password

Step 3 — Accessing Your Rooms

  1. After login you see your Student Dashboard
  2. All rooms assigned to you by your teacher appear as cards
  3. Click Enter Room to open a room
  4. You can only see rooms you have been assigned to

Inside a Room

SectionWhat to Do
LessonsClick any lesson title to read. Optional video link opens in a new tab.
MaterialsClick Download to save a file, or Open Link for external resources.
AssignmentsClick Submit before the due date. Upload your file and add optional notes. You can only submit once.
QuizzesClick Take Quiz. Answer all multiple-choice questions. Click Submit. You can only attempt each quiz once.

⚠️ Quiz Warning: Do not switch browser tabs during a quiz. Tab switching is detected, logged, and reported to your teacher automatically.

Viewing Your Grades

  1. Click My Grades in the sidebar
  2. Assignment grades show your score, teacher feedback, and date graded
  3. Quiz results show your percentage score

Announcements

  1. Click the 🔔 bell icon in the top bar to open the announcements panel
  2. New announcements have a red badge — click to read the full detail
  3. Click Mark as Read inside the popup to clear the notification
  4. You can also go to Announcements in the sidebar to see all posts

Your Profile

  1. Click your avatar in the top-right corner, or go to /accounts/profile/
  2. Edit Profile tab: Update your name, email, and profile photo
  3. Change Password tab: Enter your current password, then set a new one
  4. Grade level and username can only be changed by your teacher

3. Teacher Guide

Step 1 — Register & Get Approved

  1. Go to /accounts/register/, select Role: Teacher
  2. Complete email OTP verification
  3. Wait for the Super Admin to approve your teacher account
  4. You will receive an email with a password setup link once approved

Step 2 — Approve Student Accounts

  1. Go to Student Approvals in the sidebar (shows a badge when pending)
  2. Review each pending student's name, grade level, and email
  3. Click Approve — the student receives a password setup link by email
  4. Click Reject to permanently delete the account (student is notified by email)

📌 Tip: Check the student's Grade Level before approving. Only students with the correct grade can be assigned to matching rooms.

Step 3 — Create a Room

  1. Click Create Room in the sidebar
  2. Fill in the Room Name (e.g. "Grade 8 — Mathematics")
  3. Select the Subject and Grade Level
  4. Add a description (optional) and thumbnail image (optional)
  5. Click Create Room

Each room is locked to one subject and one grade level. Students can only be assigned to rooms matching their grade.

Step 4 — Assign Students to a Room

  1. From the Teacher Dashboard, click Students on a room card
  2. The left panel shows students already assigned
  3. The right panel shows approved students in the matching grade — click Add to assign
  4. To remove a student, click the ✕ button next to their name
  5. Assigned/removed students receive an email notification automatically

Managing Room Content

Click Open on any room card to access the Room Overview. From there:

Content TypeHow to AddNotes

MaterialsClick + Add next to MaterialsUpload a file (PDF/image) and/or add an external link
AssignmentsClick + Add next to AssignmentsSet title, description, due date, max score. Students are notified by email.
QuizzesClick + Add next to QuizzesCreate quiz first, then add MCQ questions with 4 options and mark the correct answer

Grading Submissions

  1. From the Room Overview, click the submission count next to an assignment
  2. The submissions table shows each student's file and status (Submitted / Graded)
  3. Click Grade to open the grading form
  4. Enter the score and optional feedback, then save
  5. The student receives an email with their score and feedback immediately

Posting Announcements

  1. Go to Announcements in the sidebar → click New Announcement
  2. Fill in the title and content
  3. Choose the Type (Notice, Event, Exam, Holiday, Urgent)
  4. Choose the Audience — All, Students only, Teachers only, or a specific Grade
  5. Optionally attach a file or link, schedule a future publish date, or pin the post
  6. Check Send Email to blast the announcement to all recipients by email
  7. Click Post Announcement

Teacher Profile

  1. Click your avatar or go to /accounts/teacher-profile/
  2. Upload a profile photo, update name and email
  3. Use the Change Password tab to update your password

Error Dashboard

Go to Error Dashboard in the sidebar (/system/errors/) to view and resolve any runtime errors captured by the system.

4. Super Admin Guide

The Super Admin portal is at /superadmin/. It provides full system oversight.

Approving Teacher Accounts

  1. Go to Users → filter by Role: Teacher and Status: Pending
  2. Click Approve & Send Link — the teacher receives a password setup email immediately
  3. To resend a setup link to an already-approved user, click Resend Link

📌 Important: Make sure SITE_BASE_URL is set correctly in local_settings.py before approving. The setup link URL is built from this setting.

Site Settings

  1. Go to Site Settings in the superadmin sidebar
  2. Review the current SITE_BASE_URL, email backend, and school name
  3. To change values, edit lms_project/local_settings.py and restart the server

Documents

The superadmin sidebar has links to three printable documents:

  • System Document — full technical documentation
  • Changelog — version history of all releases
  • Presentation — visual slide deck for stakeholder presentations
  • Instructions — this page

All pages have a Print / Save PDF button in the top bar.

5. Email & Password Setup

For Developers — Activating Gmail

  1. Copy lms_project/local_settings.example.py → lms_project/local_settings.py
  2. Set your ngrok URL: SITE_BASE_URL = 'https://your-url.ngrok-free.app'
  3. Enable 2FA on your Gmail account
  4. Go to: myaccount.google.com → Security → App Passwords → create one for "Mail"
  5. Paste the 16-character App Password as EMAIL_HOST_PASSWORD
  6. Set EMAIL_BACKEND = 'django.core.mail.backends.smtp.EmailBackend'
  7. Test with: python manage.py test_email your@email.com

💡 Development tip: When EMAIL_BACKEND is set to console, all emails print in your terminal window. Look for the setup URL in the output when approving a user.

6. Troubleshooting

ProblemCauseFix
Cannot log in — "pending approval" Account not yet approved Ask your teacher (student) or admin (teacher) to approve your account
Setup link says "expired" Link is older than 72 hours or already used Ask your teacher/admin to resend the setup link from the user management page
Did not receive OTP email Email in spam, or incorrect email entered Check spam folder. Click "Resend code" on the OTP page (30-second cooldown). If email was wrong, re-register.
Did not receive setup link email Email in spam, or email config not set Check spam. Ask admin to resend. If using console backend, check the terminal window.
403 Forbidden on any page Role mismatch or not logged in Log out and log in again. Make sure you are using the correct portal URL for your role.
CSRF verification failed Stale browser cookie Open DevTools → Application → Cookies → delete csrftoken and sessionid → reload page
Email not sending (Gmail) Wrong App Password or 2FA not enabled Run python manage.py test_email your@email.com and follow the error message. Ensure Gmail 2FA is ON and you are using an App Password, not your account password.
Setup link has wrong domain (localhost instead of ngrok) SITE_BASE_URL not set to ngrok URL Edit lms_project/local_settings.py and set SITE_BASE_URL to your ngrok URL, then restart the server
Room not showing students to assign Student's grade level doesn't match room's grade level Check the student's grade level in the Admin Portal → Users. It must match the room's grade level exactly.
Quiz submitted but no score shown Quiz was already attempted Each quiz can only be taken once. The score is visible in My Grades.
